**Parties:** LTV Desking PRO ("Provider") and \***\*\*\*\*\***\_\_\***\*\*\*\*\*** ("Dealer").

1. **Pilot term & scope.** 30-day pilot (renewable once by email) of the LTV Desking PRO software
   for Dealer's rooftop at **\*\***\_\_**\*\***. Up to \_\_\_ named users. Companion tooling only: the
   software produces estimates and preliminary lender screens; it does not produce contracts,
   disclosures, or credit decisions.

2. **Fees & billing.** $**\_\_** for the pilot term, invoiced directly (no auto-renewal, no card on
   file). [Owner decision: discounted-paid recommended over free — paid pilots produce honest
   feedback.]

3. **Estimates disclaimer & responsibility split.** All figures produced by the software —
   payments, taxes, fees, LTV, lender fits — are estimates based on Dealer-entered data. Dealer
   retains sole responsibility for final contract figures, consumer-credit disclosures
   (TILA/Reg Z), lender submissions, and compliance with state and federal law. Provider's AI
   features may produce inaccurate output; Dealer must verify lender program terms against source
   rate sheets before relying on them.

4. **Limitation of liability.** Provider's aggregate liability is capped at fees paid under this
   agreement; no consequential damages. [Counsel: standard carve-outs.]

5. **Data.** Dealer owns its data. Provider processes it per the Safeguards addendum (Exhibit A):
   encryption, access controls, breach notice within 72 hours, subprocessor list, retention per
   the Data Retention & Disposal Policy. On termination: CSV/JSON export within 14 days of
   request, then deletion; backups age out within 14 days thereafter.

6. **Support & availability.** Business-hours email support (response target: same business day)
   plus the in-app problem-report channel; emergency phone for outages. Commercially reasonable
   efforts; no uptime SLA during the pilot.

7. **Pilot conduct.** Dealer designates a desk-manager point of contact, agrees to the weekly
   20-minute feedback call, and permits anonymized usage analytics (no consumer PII) for the
   success metrics in the Pilot Charter.

8. **Term/termination.** Either party may end the pilot with 7 days' notice. Sections 3–5 survive.

9. **Governing law.** [Counsel: align with ToS — currently New York; Michigan may be more natural.]
